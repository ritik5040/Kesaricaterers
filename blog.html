Quick Bites

Healthy Eating Tips from Our Kitchen

Simple, actionable wisdom from our chefs that you can apply every day.

🥣

Start with Warm Water

Begin your morning with a glass of warm water and a squeeze of lemon. It kickstarts digestion and flushes toxins naturally.

🌿

Cook with Ghee, Not Oil

Pure desi ghee is rich in healthy saturated fats and fat-soluble vitamins. Used in moderation, it's far superior to refined vegetable oils.

🥗

Eat the Rainbow

Different coloured vegetables provide different phytonutrients. Aim for at least 3–4 different coloured vegetables in your daily meals.

⏰

Eat Before Sunset

Ayurveda recommends eating your heaviest meal at noon when digestive fire (Agni) is strongest, and keeping dinner light and early.
