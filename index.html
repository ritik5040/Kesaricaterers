Our Story

Built on Quality,
Trust & Care

Kesari Caters was founded on a simple yet powerful belief: food is the heart of every celebration. In a world where catering businesses often chase decoration and spectacle, we chose a different path — one where quality, taste, and the guest experience come first.

Over more than a decade, we've had the honour of serving hundreds of families across Jaipur and Rajasthan — at their most intimate moments and grandest celebrations. Every event we serve at is treated with the same care as our very first.

Meet the Visionary

The Man Behind
Every Perfect Meal

Om Prakash Sharma, the founder and head chef of Kesari Caters, built this venture from the ground up with a single-minded passion — to make every meal an unforgettable experience. With over 10 years of culinary expertise and a deep understanding of Indian food traditions, he has earned the trust of thousands of families across Rajasthan.

His philosophy is rooted in respect for every client: "A wedding happens only once. Every guest deserves to leave with a smile and the memory of exceptional food." This commitment to excellence is what sets Kesari Caters apart.
